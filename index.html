Terminology management — sample extract

A core deliverable on large-scale projects is the terminology glossary — the backbone of cross-document consistency. Below is a sample extract from the CN–SR–EN technical glossary developed during railway infrastructure work. The full glossary is available in the Technical Writing portfolio.

Chinese Serbian English Category Note
接触网 kontaktna mreža overhead contact line system Railway Refers to the full catenary system, not just the contact wire.
正线 pravilni kolosek main line track Railway Serbian distinguishes 正线 (pravilni) from 到发线 (prijemno-otpravni). Direct loan of "main track" risks ambiguity.
停电作业 rad bez napona de-energized work Safety Preferred over "power-off work" — aligns with IEC 60050 electrical safety terminology.
压光工艺 kalandriranje calendering process Manufacturing Technical textile process; not to be confused with "calendering" in printing contexts.
不动产登记机构 katastar nepokretnosti real property registry Legal Serbian "katastar" used as functional equivalent; literal back-translation to Chinese preserves legal force.
形式发票 profaktura pro forma invoice Financial Serbian "profaktura" is a standard commercial term; preferred over calque "forma faktura".
